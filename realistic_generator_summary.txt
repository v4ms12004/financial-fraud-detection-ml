COMPLICATIONS ADDED
------------------------------
1. Partial drains        : 60-95% of victim balance stolen
   Breaks: org_balance_minus_amount_gap, amount_over_oldorg

2. Balance noise         : up to 2% fee/rounding noise on 70% of txns
   Breaks: all accounting consistency features

3. Split fraud txns      : 30% of chains use 2-3 transfers
   Breaks: clean TRANSFER->CASHOUT two-step pattern

4. Legit large transfers : 2% of normal TRANSFERs take 70-99% of balance
   Breaks: amount/transfer type as a strong fraud signal

5. Legit zero drains     : 1% of normal txns fully drain sender
   Breaks: balance goes to zero = fraud heuristic
